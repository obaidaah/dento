Calender

1- Top Bar

  • in top bar area you can choose the date range of the calender - it will be ( today ) by default
  • if you want to show next or past day appoinrments press on the right and left arrows on top left are of this bar
  • if you pressed on Rooms box you can show the appointments of this room only
  • if you pressed on Doctor's box you can show the appointments of this doctor only
  • if want to see all the appointments of specific date range ( day- week - month ) press on the boutons on right side of top bar

2- Making new appointment

  • in calender page you can make new appointment by selecting the date first then you can press on left click mouse boutton in the desire time from the calender keep pressing the click until the finshing time then relase the click
  • the appointment form will show up automatically to fill the details about the patient
  • if the patient is already registred before you can choose his file by writting his name or phone or id and it will show up
  • if the patient is new you can press on ( + ) button beside the search bar on this form and fill the patient info then appointment details
  • last thing press on submit button to make this appointment

3- Chahnging details of appointment

  • If you wish to change any appointment details such as (appointment date , time , doctor , room , type ), just click on the patient's appointment, then press on ( edit ) 
  • Now in this form, you can edit any appointment details, then press on update button.
  • if you wish to change the appointment ( time , room ) on the same day as the appointment 
  • Just click on the patient's appointment and hold and drag it to another time or room  

4- Veiw appointment details

  • If you wish to view patient appointment details, click on the appointment required then the summary info about the patient will show up and if you press on ( View ) the full details of this appointment specifically, such as (operations,notes, X-ray, prescriptions, finance)  will show up.
  • If you wish to view all details about this patient, just click on his appointment then from this panel you can press on (medical, clinical,chart,X-rays,invoices,payments) to view the details.

5- Changing apointments Status

  • To change the patient's appointment status, one click on his appointment then from this panel
    In the status section,  press on which stage this patient is in.
  • You can change the appointment status from dashboard as well (in appointments section)
    There is a small pencil icon on the right side of each appointment. Press it to change the status or viewing details of this patient.

6- Alert icons on appointment

  • Every small icon that appears on appointments means different things. 
  • example #Alert for ( urgent patient , alert of specific note about this patient ,medical and clinical history, finance alert , lab order)
  • An urgent and alert note can be created on the appointment form.
  • Medical and Clinical history icons are created automatically if there is any information detailes in the patient file.
  • The lab order icon is created automatically if there is a lab order that has not been delivered to the patient yet. 
  • A financial alert is created automatically if there is an invoice on this without complete payments yet
  • If you pass your mouse over the icons, the details of these alerts will show up.
